Subscription tracking made simple and private

SubTracker is a personal subscription tracker. It helps you keep track of your recurring payments, set reminders, and securely store service credentials — all offline on your device.

Features

  • Track all your subscriptions in one place
  • Get payment reminders before due dates
  • 50+ popular service templates (Netflix, Spotify, ChatGPT, etc.)
  • Securely store credentials with Face ID / Touch ID
  • See monthly and yearly spending overview
  • Support for multiple currencies (TRY, USD, EUR)
  • English and Turkish language support
  • 100% offline — no data leaves your device

Frequently Asked Questions

Does SubTracker sell or provide subscriptions?
No. SubTracker is a personal tracking tool only. It does not sell, resell, or provide access to any subscriptions or paid services. Users manually enter their own subscription details to keep track of payments.

Is my data safe?
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device using AES-256 encryption (SQLCipher). Passwords are stored in the iOS Keychain with biometric protection. No data is ever sent to any server.

Does SubTracker require an account?
No. SubTracker works entirely offline without any account or registration.

How do I set up payment reminders?
When adding or editing a subscription, enable "Payment Reminder" and set how many days before the payment date you want to be notified.

How do I delete a subscription?
Tap on the subscription to open its details, then tap the "Delete" button.
